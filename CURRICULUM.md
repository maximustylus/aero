## 🧱 Clinical Hazards (Obstacles)
[cite_start]Obstacles represent common testing errors and safety risks that clinicians must navigate[cite: 20]:
* **Contraindication Cracks**: Absolute contraindications (e.g., unstable angina).
* **Artifact Alley**: ECG motion artifacts or signal noise.
* **Calibration Canyon**: Uncalibrated gas analyzers or flow sensors.
* **Hyperventilation Storm**: Non-metabolic respiratory overdrive.
